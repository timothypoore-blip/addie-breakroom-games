Predict. Match. Accelerate.

Addie gives discharge coordinators real-time AI intelligence inside their existing EHR — so every placement decision is faster, smarter, and safer.

Predict — Discharge Readiness AI
Flags patients 24–48h before they're ready for discharge. Gives coordinators time to arrange placements before beds back up.
Match — Ranked PAC Recommendations
AI ranks 5,000+ PAC partners by clinical fit, insurance coverage, real-time bed availability, and historical outcomes. 92% match accuracy.
Accelerate — Automated Referral Workflows
Send referral packets, track responses, and confirm placements — all inside Epic, Cerner, or MEDITECH. Zero extra logins.
EHR-Native Integration
Epic App Orchard certified. Works natively inside your existing clinical workflows — no new UI, no change management.

Specialty Programs

Beyond the Standard Transition

Addie supports specialized care models that require more than a standard referral — with tailored workflows for each program type.

🏛️

iSNP Programs

Institutional Special Needs Plans coordination — connecting facility-based SNP members with the right long-term care placements and ensuring plan visibility throughout the transition.

🤝

PACE Integration

Program of All-inclusive Care for the Elderly — Addie integrates PACE program referral flows, enabling hospitals to connect members with PACE centers for community-based long-term care.

🗂️

Care Management Groups

Complex care management coordination for high-utilizer patients — bridging discharge planning with ongoing community-based care management teams and social determinants support.

🏡

Hospital at Home

Supports hospital-to-home acute care transitions — coordinating home monitoring equipment, nursing visits, and clinical follow-up for patients discharged under Hospital at Home programs.
