How Addie Came to Be

The Discharge Board Shouldn't Be a Whiteboard.

Post-acute care transitions are one of the most consequential — and most broken — workflows in American healthcare. Discharge coordinators were managing complex patient placements with fax machines, phone calls, and spreadsheets while beds backed up in the ED.

PEP Inc. was founded to fix exactly that. We built Addie — an AI-powered platform that connects hospitals, post-acute providers, and health plans in real time — so that the right patient gets to the right place as fast as medically possible.

We're not an EHR replacement. We're not another referral directory. Addie is a living network — one that gets smarter with every placement, every outcome, every discharge.

The Addie Journey
From Whiteboard to AI Platform
2020
Problem identified. PEP Inc. founders document the scope of post-acute placement failures across 6 health systems.
2021
First prototype. Addie v0.1 pilots at a 300-bed community hospital — reducing average boarding time by 31% in 60 days.
2022
PAC network launches. 500+ SNF, IRF, and home health partners join the Addie network in Year 1.
2023
Epic integration. Addie becomes EHR-native — discharge coordinators never leave their existing workflow.
2024
Payer network opens. Health plans join Addie to gain transition visibility and reduce avoidable readmissions.
2025+
5,000+ PAC partners. 92% AI match accuracy. Expanding into iSNP, PACE, and Hospital at Home.

What We Believe

Our Operating Principles

The values that guide every product decision, partnership, and hire.

Patient First, Always

Every algorithm, every workflow, every product decision is filtered through one question: does this get the patient to the right place faster and more safely?

Radical Transparency

We show hospitals which PAC partners declined referrals and why. We show PAC providers their quality scores. Transparency builds better networks.

Built to Integrate

Addie doesn't ask hospitals to change workflows. We plug into Epic, Cerner, and MEDITECH natively — because adoption only happens when change is invisible.

Trust Through Compliance

HIPAA-aligned by design. Every data flow, every integration, every API call is built with protected health information standards as the baseline — not an afterthought.

Speed is Clinical

Every hour a patient waits for placement is a clinical event. We optimize for time-to-placement because we understand that speed isn't a feature — it's a care outcome.

Network Effects, Shared

Every hospital that joins makes matches better for PAC partners. Every PAC partner that joins improves outcomes for patients. We build the network — you benefit from it.
